Patch 0.0.41

Calamari Beta Testing, often known as CBT, is a videogame...

Welcome to Patch 0.04 of the Calamari Beta Test. We've been making a lot of refinements based on player feedback, and this patch brings some major adjustments to healing, cover mechanics, range modifiers, progression, and more. Let's dive into the details.

-Medkit Revamps

After the last playtest, we noticed that the lack of medics turned deep wounds into a major hassle. To address this, we're adjusting how medical kits function to allow all players to carry and use them.

  • Standard First Aid Kits (FAKs) now have 3 uses, primarily treating flesh wounds. If only deep wounds exist, they reduce severity by converting them into flesh wounds.
  • Surgical Kits have been replaced with Advanced First Aid Kits (AFAKs), which are no longer exclusive to medics and are used to treat deep wounds.

-Cover Rebalancing

Cover has always been tricky to balance, so we've simplified the system:

  • All cover now provides a +1 bonus, regardless of type.
  • Weapon penetration values now dictate effectiveness against cover.
  • Penetration (Pen) 2 and 3 weapons can negate cover bonuses entirely based on cover type.

-Range Modifier Consistency

Range modifiers have been inconsistent, so we're refining them for clarity:

  • Close range: <15m
  • Medium range: 15-50m
  • Long range: 50-100m
  • Extreme range: 100m+

These changes should better reflect actual combat distances. We will continue to monitor and adjust as needed.

-Achievements and Progression

Originally, achievements were just for fun, but players suggested they should impact progression. We agree.

  • Achievements now contribute to skill unlocks and player progression.
  • Cosmetic rewards are being added to make them more enticing.

-Ammo Tracking Adjustments

Traditional ammo tracking is tedious and slows gameplay. Instead, we're experimenting with a new approach:

  • Rather than tracking every bullet, we track special ammo uses (bursting, suppressive fire, spraying, mag dumping).
  • Each special use has a limited number of times it can be performed before a forced reload.
  • Suppressive fire now has a turn limit before requiring a reload.

This system will be tested in the next CBT to see if it improves gameplay fluidity.

-Final Notes

Thank you to all our playtesters for your feedback and support. Everything you provide helps us refine and improve the game.

As always, send feedback in the ⁠calamari-discussion channel. Happy testing!

-Spiny, Lead Developer and Designer

Patch Notes 0.0.41

Bug Fixes:

  • Fixed an issue where range modifiers sometimes failed to trigger correctly.
  • Fixed penetration calculations for certain weapons.
  • Fixed a bug where some deep wounds were not being converted properly with FAKs.

Gameplay Changes:

  • All cover now provides a +1 defense bonus.
  • Weapon penetration values reworked to interact properly with cover.
  • Rebalanced medkits to be usable by all classes.
  • Adjusted range categories for consistency.
  • Overhauled ammo tracking to focus on special use cases rather than individual bullets.

Improvements and Additions:

  • Achievements now contribute to progression and skill unlocks.
  • Cosmetic rewards added to achievement system.
  • Implemented Advanced First Aid Kits (AFAKs) to replace Surgical Kits.
  • Added a new system for tracking ammo usage during bursts and suppressive fire.
